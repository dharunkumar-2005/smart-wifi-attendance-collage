## 📸 Camera Tips for Best Results

### Lighting
- ✅ Use natural light from a window
- ✅ Face should be well-lit
- ❌ Avoid backlighting (sun behind you)
- ❌ Avoid harsh shadows on face

### Positioning
- ✅ Position camera at eye level
- ✅ Face centered in frame guide
- ✅ Entire face visible (forehead to chin)
- ❌ Don't close eyes or smile awkwardly
- ❌ Don't tilt head excessively

### Device
- ✅ Clean camera lens before capturing
- ✅ Ensure camera is not being used by other apps
- ✅ Grant camera permissions when prompted
- ❌ Don't cover camera
- ❌ Don't have other apps using camera

## 🎥 Camera Permission Error?

### Error: "Camera access denied"
**Solution:**
1. Look for permission request popup (⚠️ may be hidden)
2. Click "Allow" or "Grant" permission
3. Refresh page and try again
4. Check browser camera settings

### Error: "Camera not available"
**Solution:**
1. Close other apps using camera (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
2. Ensure camera is not in use
3. Restart browser
4. Try on different device

### Error: "Browser doesn't support camera"
**Solution:**
1. Use supported browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
2. Update to latest browser version
3. Use device with camera
4. Contact admin for alternative submission

## ❓ FAQs

**Q: Can I retake my photo?**
A: Yes! You can click "RETAKE PHOTO" as many times as you want before submitting. Once submitted, face is locked.

**Q: What if my photo is blurry?**
A: Click "RETAKE PHOTO" and try again. Ensure better lighting and position.

**Q: Can I submit from a different device?**
A: No. Each device can only submit once and gets locked. Contact admin if you need to submit from different device.

**Q: What if I lost my registered device?**
A: Contact admin with your registration number. They can unlock your device binding.

**Q: Will my face photo be shared?**
A: No. Face photos are stored securely and visible only to authorized staff for verification.

**Q: Can I change the device I use?**
A: Only with admin approval. They'll send an OTP for device change verification.

**Q: What if camera permissions were denied?**
A: Check browser settings → Permissions → Camera, then Allow for this site. Refresh and retry.
